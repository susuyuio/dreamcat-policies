Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We do not actively collect sensitive personal private information. When users interact with our TikTok official account, we may automatically receive public data provided by TikTok Platform, including public username, interaction records, likes, comments, shared content, and public behavioral data. For users who purchase premium content or unlock content through advertisements, we may store transaction verification records and platform-generated order data for service confirmation.

2. Use of Collected Information

Analyze audience preferences to optimize comic, drama, and storytelling content.

Maintain normal account operation, user interaction, and message replies.

Verify payment records, premium content access, and advertisement-based unlocking services.

Prevent abnormal traffic, fraud, and unauthorized access to paid resources.

3. Information Sharing & Third Parties

We will never sell, rent, trade, or leak your personal information for commercial purposes. Data may be temporarily shared with authorized third-party service providers, including payment processors and advertising partners, solely for transaction completion, advertisement display, and service fulfillment. All third parties are required to comply with international data protection regulations.

4. Cookies & Platform Data

We do not deploy independent cookies or tracking tools. All user data storage, transmission, and encryption comply with TikTok official data privacy regulations. Behavioral tracking is fully controlled and limited by the TikTok platform.

5. Minor Protection

Some fictional drama and comic scenes contain dramatic plots that are not suitable for young children. We do not intentionally collect personal data from users under the age of 13. Minors must access our content under the supervision and consent of a legal guardian. We strictly restrict underage users from accessing paid services.

6. Data Security

As an enterprise entity, we adopt standard data encryption and security protection measures to protect user interaction records and transaction data. We take reasonable technical steps to prevent data leakage, misuse, or unauthorized dissemination.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to comply with platform rules and commercial service requirements. The latest version will be permanently published on our official public link page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding data privacy, information storage, or policy clauses, please contact our official team through TikTok direct message.
